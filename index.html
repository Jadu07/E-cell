About Us

Sharks Sphere, the Entrepreneurship Cell of Newton School of Technology, fosters an
entrepreneurial spirit among students and ignites innovation. We nurture budding ideas, guiding
them from conception to impactful ventures through mentorship, networking, and funding
opportunities. By connecting student-led enterprises with incubators, seed investors, and
industry leaders, we empower aspiring entrepreneurs to transform ideas into successful
startups, shaping the future of technology and business.
