Our Vision

The Entrepreneurship Cell (E-Cell) at Newton School of Technology fosters innovation, creativity,
and entrepreneurial thinking. We provide a platform for aspiring entrepreneurs to develop ideas, gain
industry insights, and connect with mentors and investors. Through workshops, hackathons, and
networking events, we bridge the gap between academics and real-world business. Our mission is to
empower students with an entrepreneurial mindset, driving technological advancements, creating
sustainable solutions, and shaping future innovators and leaders.

What Makes Us Different?
