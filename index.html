Our Vision

The Entrepreneurship Cell (E-Cell) at Newton School of Technology aims to foster a culture of
innovation, creativity, and entrepreneurial thinking among students. Our vision is to create a thriving
ecosystem where aspiring entrepreneurs can develop their ideas, gain industry insights, and
transform their vision into successful ventures.

We strive to provide a platform that nurtures startups, connects students with mentors and investors,
and equips them with the necessary skills to navigate the challenges of entrepreneurship. Through
workshops, hackathons, networking events, and speaker sessions, we aspire to bridge the gap
between academic learning and real-world business opportunities.

Our ultimate goal is to empower students with an entrepreneurial mindset, enabling them to drive
technological advancements, create sustainable business solutions, and contribute to the startup
ecosystem. The E-Cell at Newton School of Technology envisions itself as a launchpad for future,
innovators and leaders, shaping a new generation of problem-solvers and changemakers.
